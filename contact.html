Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about working with Stackly.

We typically kick off new engagements within 1-2 weeks of contract signing. Our streamlined onboarding process includes a discovery workshop, team introductions, and project setup — ensuring we hit the ground running from day one.
Timelines vary based on scope: Starter projects take 4-8 weeks, Growth engagements 3-6 months, and Enterprise transformations 6-12+ months. We provide detailed timelines during our discovery phase and use agile methodologies for incremental delivery.
We serve organizations of all sizes — from ambitious startups to Fortune 500 enterprises. Our engagement models are flexible and scalable, designed to meet you where you are and grow with your ambitions.
Every engagement includes a post-launch support period (30-90 days). We also offer ongoing maintenance retainers, 24/7 support, security patching, and continuous optimization to ensure your digital assets remain performant and secure long-term.
We sign NDAs before every engagement and maintain strict information barriers between client accounts. Our SOC2-certified infrastructure, role-based access controls, and encrypted communications ensure your sensitive data remains fully protected.
